This GPA Calculator is intended for use by students of the Federal University of Petroleum Resources, Effurun (FUPRE), Delta State. The database of courses used is based on the 2017/2018 academic session for the College of Science and 2018/2019 academic session for the College of Technology. Note that the course details may not be exact with your course structure

How to use this GPA Calculator:

  • On the Intro screen, select your college and department.
  • Select the level(s) and semester(s) that you want to calculate for.
  • Click Proceed.
  • On the Semester screen, all the course details for the level(s) and semester(s) you selected would be displayed. You can navigate semesters by swiping left or right or you could use the navigation buttons at the bottom of the screen:
    • Select your grade for each course.
    • You may edit the Credit Units if need be.
    • You could leave a Credit Unit textfield blank.
    • Click on the delete button on any course if you do not wish to calculate that particular course.
    • You may decide to add more courses to calculate for each semester by using the + button on the menu bar.
      (This feature would be useful for carryover courses).
  • Click Calculate.
  • The result of the calculation is then displayed.

The Credit Point (CP) of each course is calculated by:

CP = Credit Unit (CU) Ã— Rating

The Total Number of Units (TNU) is gotten by summing the CU of all the courses used in the calculation.

The Total Credit Point (TCP) is gotten by summing the CP of all the courses used in the calculation.

The Grade Point Average (GPA) then calculated:
GPA = TCP / TNU

For Cumulative GPA (CGPA) calculation:

The summation of every TNU is gotten from every semester from beginning to the current semester as Cummulative Number of Units (CNU). While the summation of every TCP is gotten as Cummulative Credit Point (CCP).
Then the CGPA is calculated:
CGPA = CCP / CNU
